例题：（19交大）

Give your opinions on this perspective: Obstacles can be seen as opportunities.

样文：

Life is not all rosy. We will all undergo ups and downs; joys and sorrows. Major obstacles in our lives, such as physical handicaps, failures in our career and loss of beloved ones can be terrifying. But optimists tend to regard obstacles as opportunities. Indeed, lots of strong-willed persons can emerge from barriers in their lives wiser and tougher.

Let’s explore how we can transform obstacles into opportunities from different angles. To start with, physical handicap can be devastating to most people, nevertheless lot can be derived from it from the perspective of a fearless person. Helen Keller suffered from irreversible sight and hearing impairment at an early age, but she ended up as a fertile writer who savored the manifold blessings of lives more than we physically sound persons. Equally remarkably, athletes who attend the Paralympics can sometimes run and swim faster than an average person without physical disabilities.

Incredible stories like theses are no more rarely seen among ordinary people. Student who failed in their academic study in primary schools would perform much better and achieve a lot eventually if they were prodded and inspired timely. With humility and perseverance, slow-witted students are more likely to be aware of their own weaknesses and be motivated to be successful.

Last but not the least, think about how much our country has been through in the past two decades. Massive earthquakes and outbreaks of epidemics once seemed to be hurdles impossible to be clear. But these hurdles turned out to be valuable opportunities for us to witness how united and capable we can be in the relentless struggles against disasters.

Granted, some obstacles in our lives can be overwhelming. But keep this in mind: what didn’t kill us makes us stronger. Only if obstacles are reckoned as an opportunities could we complete missions impossible in our lives.

Let’s shift our attention to the opposite /converse view.

样文：

It is increasingly apparent that overseas purchasing via online agents are now gaining considerable popularity. The ongoing trend appeals to some people on grounds of its convenience and affordability, whereas others are skeptical of it.

There are some distinct advantages when it comes to the process. First, convenience. the consumers can simply log on the overseas purchasing websites and have instant access to a wide array of high-quality products by simply tapping on the screen of their smart phones. Consequently, the shopping experience becomes time-saving and efficient, not to mention the fact that some commodities are not even available to domestic consumers. Second, affordability. The website can attract many consumers simultaneously to the same designer product so that they can sell it at a wholesale price, which in some cases could be only a fraction of the original one.

By contrast, many consumers voice their concerns. They would argue that the online agents cannot guarantee the quality of commodity unless the consumers check it out in person. Another worry is that sensitive personal data, such as credit card number and mobile phone number, can be leaked during the process, which might bring about security concerns.

From where I stand, pros outweigh cons. Granted, overseas purchasing is a sphere where further improvements are still highly anticipated. The trend is now becoming unstoppable and the sellers on the platform are making every effort to refine it. Nonetheless, both the sellers and buyers should deliberate on how to tackle the potential data leak generated by the new mode of buying.

样文：

Education is an essential not an extra. It is an intrinsic right of each one of us rather than the privilege of a few. Then how could we ensure as many students as possible the easy and quick access to education? Attending online course should not be the last resort. Quite the contrary, online eduction is now an accelerating trend. It amazes us to see that students worldwide can attend Massive Open Online Courses (MOOC) and obtain the information they need.

There are many aspects contributing to the popularity of online courses. First, diversity. There are a wide span of courses available online which range from literature to economics and from elementary courses to advanced ones. You can browse the relevant information on open courses websites effortlessly. The merit of online courses is not confined to this. Let’s take a look at the accessibility of such course. Lots of prestigious universities, including Harvard, offer MOOC to students worldwide and some of them are free of charge. To catch a glimpse of the charisma of elite scholars there, you don’t need to travel long distance, Instead, you could instantly watch informative lectures at home by a simple click of the mouse. Last, completing daily routines online is now prevailing. Think about online shopping and online dating. Students might not do without online courses lest they should fall behind the times. Limitation, benefits and drawbacks

Undeniably, more and more attention is being paid to this new mode of learning. The role of online education in our lives is literally not negligible. It is anticipated that in the foreseeable future a growing proportion of students can benefit considerably from it.

例题：Write an essay on what human beings have learned about the quick spread of a virus and what should be done to deal with it.

样文：

Urgent action is now being taken around the globe to combat the novel coronavirus at present. The epidemic is still raging in spite of it being effectively contained in China. Throughout human history, how to fight against a virus has invariably been a thorny problem.

What might account for the swift spread of the virus ? There are many contributing factors. Before anything else, human beings have had very limited knowledge in this sphere. It is no surprising that mankind had long been afflicted by each breakout. Secondly, the increase in the world population has been exponential and the whole world has been much more closely connected than ever before. People travel by air and by train very frequently and effortlessly due to considerable improvement in public transit home and broad. And the increased mobility makes it easier and faster for the virus to be spread from person to person and from country to country.

Much can be done to tackle the spread of the virus. To quarantine residents in the virus-hit areas is a promising start. Decreased mobility had been proven to be the simplest and most effective measure to contain the spread in human history. Priority should also be given to how the medical resources can be mobilized in the rescue mission. In China, hundreds of thousands of medics have been dispatched to the infected areas and 1000-bed hospitals have been build within ten days to treat patients. More factories are being called on to manufacture masks and the daily capacity now increases to over 50 millions masks a day. Another concern is that people should be made aware of the potential exposure to viruses again if they still recklessly eat the meat of some wild animals. Last but not the least, more medical experts should dedicate themselves to the development of vaccines against viruses. The process can be time-consuming and strenuous. But we are convinced that the dawn of victory will appear eventually provided that we never relent in our efforts.
